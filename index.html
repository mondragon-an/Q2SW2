Hello Kitty, whose full name is Kitty White is a cat who is 5 apples tall and weighs 3 apples. She has a twin sister Mimmy, and likes baking. "You can never have too many friends!"
Cinnamoroll, is a dog who was born in the clouds. He likes eating and has the special skill of being able to fly with his ears. "Let's fly like fluffy clouds~"
Pompompurin, is 5 big puddings tall and weighs 1 watermelon. He’s very good at napping and dreams of becoming even bigger. "Drink milk, eat pudding, have some snacks~"
My Melody, is very honest good natured and kind. She likes to bake almond pound cakes and wearing the pink hood her grandmother made for her. "Yaaan!"
Kuromi, was born on October 31st which is Halloween. She's fond of making mischeif and she's very cheeky but charming. "Say hello to my little friend!"
